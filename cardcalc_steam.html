Guide

If you follow the required settings and methods, the recognition will be completed in one minute.

※ Set in-game required options ※

1. Resolution : 1920x1080 Or 2560x1440 Or 2560x1080 Or 3440x1440

2. [Force 21:9 Aspect Ratio] uncheck

3. Sort: Name (In ascending order) [ABC···.]

4. Brigthness : 50%(recommend)
If it is too bright or too dark, may not be recognized or incorrect.
Also, please turn off the NVDIA filter and proceed.


※ How to take a picture ※

1. Pictures taken with the in-game screenshot function
(PrintScreenCapture key above the Insert key)

2. No duplication for each picture (prevent misrecognition)
Ex. If the last card "Banda" was taken in the first picture, take a picture of "Vertus", the line below it,
and repeat this process until the last card
